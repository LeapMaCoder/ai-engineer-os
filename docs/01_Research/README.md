# 01_Research

## Purpose

Capture evidence that informs Product decisions.

Research feeds Product; it does **not** authorize coding.

## Contains

- Competitor analysis (Boot.dev, Duolingo, Codecademy, LeetCode, DataCamp, Mimo, etc.)
- User / persona research
- Market and positioning notes
- Technology spikes (time-boxed; conclusions may spawn ADRs later)

## Rules

- Prefer dated research notes with sources.
- Distinguish fact, hypothesis, and recommendation.
- Archive stale research rather than silently overwriting.
